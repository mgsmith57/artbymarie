WATERCOLOURS
Having started to learn the techniques of watercolour painting she was nonplussed in discovering her interpretation of the scenery she was painting.
With hindsight she realised that her translation conveyed a deep, vibrant and somehow serene reproduction. Perhaps it is a projection of a deep yearning.
She leaves you to be the judge.
